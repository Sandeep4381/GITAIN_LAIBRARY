Central Library

The Library of GITA has an excellent well stocked computerized library with all the required reading materials. The Library started functioning since 2004 and has gradually increasing capacity. The library is enriched with book of Academic interest, Journal, Literature and others. It is also an associate institutional member of the NDLI, INFLIBNET (E-SODHSHINDHU), DELNET, by which the students have free access to the World Wide Web having more than 200 libraries in 20 states of India and 5 countries outside India and also member of different Consortium of e-journals like SCIENCE DIRECT, IEEE, SPINGER, K- HUB and also NPTEL course materials are available for student access. DSPACE software we used for Institutional Repository. We also are providing plagiarism check through TURNITIN software..
